FAQ

Is there a minimum rank requirement?

Any player at any rank (even unranked players) are assigned a coach who will be able to elevate there game in someway, no one is too low to start!

What hours is the coaching availiable?

We will assign you a coach in the same region/timezone as you. However if you ever want coaching at extreme hours a coach from another region can always step in!

How do i become a coach?

During the signup process, you choose wether you want to be a coach or a student. We encourage anyone to try and coach!

How do i know it will work?

If after 1 month of coaching you see no increase in MMR we offer a full refund!(rank is just a number though!)
